Smart Appointment Scheduling System

We make appointment management seamless and efficient for both patients and doctors:

  • Search for doctors based on specialty and availability.
  • AI-powered assistants suggest the best time slots for appointments.
  • Receive SMS/email notifications for confirmations, reminders, and cancellations.
  • Waitlist system notifies patients when earlier slots become available.
  • Doctors can approve, reschedule, or decline appointments through their dashboard.

Virtual Consultation & Medical Record System

Our platform enables secure and convenient virtual consultations:

  • Secure video conferencing for doctor-patient consultations.
  • Access to patients' medical history and previous prescriptions.
  • Electronic prescriptions for medications.
  • Patients can download and share medical reports as PDFs.
  • Notifications for new test results or prescriptions.

Pharmacy & Medicine Availability System

We help patients find nearby pharmacies and check medicine availability:

  • Search for pharmacies by location and operating hours.
  • Pharmacies can update their available medications and stock levels.
  • Compare prices for prescribed medicines.
  • Request medicine delivery or pickup from listed pharmacies.
  • Navigation directions to the nearest pharmacy.

Health Education & Emergency Assistance

We provide reliable health information and quick emergency support:

  • Access a library of health articles and wellness videos.
  • Symptom Checker: A basic AI feature to suggest whether a user should see a doctor.
  • Emergency Contact System: Save emergency contacts and notify them quickly in case of an emergency.
  • First Aid Guide: Step-by-step instructions for common health emergencies.
  • Community Forum: Ask health-related questions and get responses from doctors or other users.

At MediConnect, we are committed to making healthcare accessible, efficient, and reliable for everyone.
